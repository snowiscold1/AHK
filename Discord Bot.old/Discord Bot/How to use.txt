To use this program:

Put your Discord server ID in the discord_server_id field of settings.json.
•To get your Discord server ID, see here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6dqYctHmazc

Put your admin role ID in the admin_role_id field of settings.json.
•To get your admin role ID see the same video here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6dqYctHmazc but instead, right click on the role and copy the ID from there.

Put your Discord bot token in the bot_token field of settings.json.
•To get a bot token, go here: https://discord.com/developers/applications then follow the tutorial here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b61kcgfOm_4 up to the 1:22 mark. 
Then, scroll down and check PRESENCE INTENT and SERVER MEMBERS INTENT and MESSAGE CONTENT INTENT

From there you can invite the bot to your server with this link:
Your 'bot ID' is your application ID found on that website.
https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=REPLACE_THIS_WITH_YOUR_BOT_ID&permissions=0&scope=bot%20applications.commands

When you invite the bot to your server it is best to give it Admin to make sure it has permissions.

Discord bot commands:
!help
!start
!stop
